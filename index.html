About the Conference

The China Southeastern Algebraic Geometry Symposium VII will be held at Sun Yat-sen University in Guangzhou on December 7–8, 2024. The conference brings together leading experts and young scholars in algebraic geometry to share recent progress and exchange ideas.

Venue: Room 415, Building 266, 135 Xingang Xi Road, Haizhu District, Guangzhou 510275, Guangdong
